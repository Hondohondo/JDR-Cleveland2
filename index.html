JDR Home Improvement — Short History

Hi, I'm Goran!

I’m the owner and founder of JDR Home Improvements. JDR Home Improvements is a small home-remodeling construction company I established in 2007. I am a Civil Engineer by trade and studied Architecture in Yugoslavia before moving to the United States. Before establishing JDR Home Improvements, I worked as crew foreman for a large residential remodeling company, and prior to that I worked in both commercial and residential construction.

We are a general contracting company and our workforce has over 30 years of experience in all aspects of home remodeling, new construction and maintenance. Our job tomorrow depends on how we do our work today.

Every job, no matter how big, is approached with same respect and professionalism. Since early 2007 our work has been based solely on referrals. We guarantee all our work and customer communication is of great importance to us. We are patient, and we aim to perform above expectations at all times.

Preliminary estimates are free and detailed estimates and all pricing is explained in the process of interviewing and drawing up the proposal. We use only licensed Plumbers, Electricians and HVAC trades. All other work we perform ourselves.

All of us at JDR Home Improvements love what we do, and take great pride in the finished product. We are open to working with any budget and will find creative ways to making our customers' visions come to life.
